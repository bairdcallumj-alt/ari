Reading lesson

The Original Story

Read the simple story of King Arthur. This story helps you understand the ideas behind Rewa Kings.

Read the Story

Open each part. Read the five sentences. Then answer the true or false questions.

1. Britain Needs a King

Long ago, Britain did not have a good king. People argued and many towns were unsafe. A wise man named Merlin wanted to help. He believed the right king would come soon. Everyone waited for a sign.

True or False

Choose one answer for each statement.

Britain already had a good king.

Merlin wanted to help.

2. The Sword in the Stone

A sword was stuck inside a stone. Many strong people tried to pull it out. They all failed. Arthur tried quietly and the sword came free. Everyone was surprised.

True or False

The sword was inside a stone.

Arthur could not move the sword.

3. Camelot

Arthur became king. He built a great castle called Camelot. Brave knights came to help him. They wanted to protect the people. The kingdom became happier.

True or False

Camelot was a castle.

The knights wanted to hurt the people.

4. The Round Table

The knights sat at a round table. No one sat at the top. Everyone was treated fairly. They solved problems together. They helped people across the land.

True or False

The knights sat at a round table.

Only one knight mattered.

5. The End of Camelot

One day the kingdom began to break apart. Friends stopped trusting each other. Camelot slowly came to an end. Arthur was remembered as a brave king. His story became a legend.

True or False

Friends stopped trusting each other.

Arthur was forgotten.
